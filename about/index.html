About

My name is Dave Herman. I’ve served on TC39, the standards committee responsible for evolving JavaScript, since 2006, and founded Mozilla Research in 2011. At Mozilla, I advised and contributed to the development of Rust, the parallel browser engine Servo, asm.js, and WebAssembly. I’ve recently joined LinkedIn’s build tools team, where I’m looking at finding new ways to help developers dare to be ambitious.
